LinkedIn Post Studio — Write better LinkedIn posts directly inside LinkedIn.

A free, lightweight Chrome extension that adds a formatting toolbar directly into LinkedIn's post editor. No external tools, no copy-paste — just open the LinkedIn post composer and start formatting.

KEY FEATURES:

• Inline Formatting — Bold, italic, and bold-italic using Unicode characters that work natively on LinkedIn
• Bullet Lists — One-click bullet points, arrows, dashes, and check marks
• Separators — Line, dot, star, and wave dividers to structure your posts
• Post Templates — Save and reuse writing blocks for hooks, CTAs, hashtags, and post structures
• Live Preview — See how your post will look on desktop and mobile before publishing
• Character & Word Counter — Real-time stats while you write
• Keyboard Shortcuts — Ctrl+B for bold, Ctrl+I for italic
• Template Import/Export — Share templates as JSON files with your team

BUILT-IN TEMPLATES:

The extension ships with starter templates for:
- Post hooks (questions, surprising stats)
- Calls to action (engagement, follow)
- Hashtag sets (tech, career)
- Post structures (listicle, story post)

Plus, you can create and save your own custom templates.

PRIVACY-FIRST:

• No data collection — everything stays on your device
• No analytics or tracking
• Minimal permissions — only storage and activeTab
• No account required
• 100% free, no premium tier

HOW IT WORKS:

1. Install the extension
2. Go to LinkedIn and open the post editor
3. The formatting toolbar appears automatically above the editor
4. Select text and click Bold, Italic, or other formatting buttons
5. Use the Preview button to see how your post will look
6. Use Templates to quickly insert saved content blocks

This extension is perfect for LinkedIn creators, marketers, thought leaders, and anyone who wants their posts to stand out with clean formatting.
