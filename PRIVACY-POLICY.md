# Privacy Policy — LinkedIn Post Studio

**Last updated:** March 5, 2026

## Overview

LinkedIn Post Studio is a Chrome extension that adds formatting tools to the LinkedIn post editor. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the extension handles and how.

## Data Collection

**LinkedIn Post Studio does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

All data created by the extension (templates, settings, usage counters) is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API (`chrome.storage.local`). This data never leaves your device.

## What We Don't Do

- We do **not** collect any personal or identifying information
- We do **not** track your browsing activity or LinkedIn usage
- We do **not** transmit any data to external servers
- We do **not** use analytics, telemetry, or tracking scripts
- We do **not** use cookies
- We do **not** require account creation or sign-in
- We do **not** access your LinkedIn credentials, messages, or connections

## Permissions Used

| Permission | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| `storage` | Save your custom templates and extension settings locally on your device |
| `activeTab` | Inject the formatting toolbar into the LinkedIn post editor on the current tab |
| `host_permissions` (linkedin.com) | Restrict the extension to only operate on LinkedIn pages |

## Local Data Stored

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

- **Templates**: Custom post templates you create (hooks, CTAs, hashtags, structures)
- **Settings**: Extension preferences (toolbar enabled, keyboard shortcuts, stats display)
- **Usage counters**: Simple counts of posts formatted and templates used (never transmitted)

You can clear all extension data at any time by removing the extension from Chrome or clearing its storage via `chrome://extensions/`.

## Third-Party Services

LinkedIn Post Studio makes **no external network requests**. It does not communicate with any servers, APIs, or third-party services.

## Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.
